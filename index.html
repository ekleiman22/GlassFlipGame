There are n glasses on the table, all standing upside down. In one move, you are allowed to turn over exactly n − 1 of them. Determine all values of n for which all the glasses can be turned up, and outline an algorithm that does this in the minimum number of moves.(This puzzle is from Algorithmic Puzzles – Anany Levitin, Maria Levitin)
Rule: for to flip all glasses but one do click on a glass that should not be flipped
